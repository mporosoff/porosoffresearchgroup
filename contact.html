Join the Group

We are always looking for motivated graduate students, postdocs, and undergraduates who are excited about catalysis, sustainable chemistry, and AI-driven materials discovery. The group offers a collaborative, interdisciplinary environment at the intersection of experiment and computation.

Graduate students — Apply through the University of Rochester Department of Chemical & Sustainability Engineering graduate program. Feel free to reach out to Prof. Porosoff directly with your CV and a brief description of your research interests.

Undergraduates — UR undergraduates interested in research opportunities should contact Prof. Porosoff directly via email.

Postdocs — We are currently recruiting a postdoctoral researcher for our ARPA-E CATALYST project on AI-driven CO2 conversion. Prospective postdocs can apply using the button below.
